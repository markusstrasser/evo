# Text

Photosynthesis is the process where plants convert {{c1::light energy}} from the sun into {{c2::chemical energy}} stored in {{c3::glucose}}.

The equation is: {{c4::6CO₂ + 6H₂O + light → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂}}

This process occurs in the {{c5::chloroplasts}} of plant cells.

# Notes

- c1: light energy (from the sun)
- c2: chemical energy (stored in glucose)
- c3: glucose (sugar molecule)
- c4: chemical equation (carbon dioxide + water + light → glucose + oxygen)
- c5: chloroplasts (organelles containing chlorophyll)
