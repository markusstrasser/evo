# Question

Identify the highlighted chamber of the heart.

# Notes

The heart has four chambers:
- Upper chambers (atria) receive blood
- Lower chambers (ventricles) pump blood
- Right side: deoxygenated blood from body → lungs
- Left side: oxygenated blood from lungs → body
